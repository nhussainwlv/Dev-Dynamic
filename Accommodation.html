How can you apply?

For new students

You can apply for an Accommodation when you have offically registered for a course on the evision system after that all that is needed is your student number.

What is available?

To live in an Accomodation you can choose between ensuite or standard in the different cities Telford, Walsall and Wolverhampton to ensure that you can get better access and understanding of the offers that we provide: Click here to see more!

Accommodation Fully secured!

When you apply for a room in hall within your a accommodation you would need to do advanced payment for it to be secured. To do this you would need to create an account on the website star Rez.
