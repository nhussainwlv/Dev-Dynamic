Open Days at University of Wolverhampton

Applied to University of Wolverhampton, or interested in studying here? Attend an open day event at our univeristy and see what we have to offer. All prospective students are welcome to our undergraduate and postgraduate open days, where you can find out more information about our courses and life at our university.

Learn more about your course!

The University of Wolverhampton offers a large variety of full-time, part-time and online courses. Applicants can choose from a range of undergraduate and postgraduate degrees, with specialisations in areas such as business, engineering, law, computing and media. Vocational qualifications in line with industry standards are also offered here! We strive to provide an engaging learning environment while offering the necessary support to ensure student success. Click here to view a full list of the courses we offer!

What to expect from us?

With campuses in Wolverhampton, Walsall and Telford, we offer a wide range of excellent facilities to support your studies. The University of Wolverhampton's City Campus is in the heart of Wolverhampton city centre. Explore our University campus and enjoy all that city life has to offer and start your journey by booking an open day today! Walsall Campus is home to our sports, teaching and many of our health-related courses. Telford Campus is home to UWRacing, where engineering and healthcare courses are taught, and innovation is king.
